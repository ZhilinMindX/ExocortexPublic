## EXOCORTEX ANALYSIS & CROSS-REFERENCES

1. **Assume nothing.**
   Every assumption is a vulnerability. Verify everything.
   **Directive Link:** #16 Never equate what is merely similar — Verify before trusting patterns.

2. **Never go against your gut.**
   Intuition is subconscious pattern recognition. The Anomaly Detector (#40) is gut feeling made systematic.
   **Directive Link:** #40 Anomaly Detector — Statistical intuition.

3. **Everyone is potentially under opposition control.**
   Trust is a vulnerability. The Exocortex assumes no session is secure until verified.
   **Directive Link:** #39 Session Handshake — Verify state before proceeding.

4. **Don't look back; you are never completely alone.**
   The past is a trap. Focus on the present state vector.
   **Directive Link:** #12 The Solution usually is hidden in the problem — Look at the current problem, not past failures.

5. **Go with the flow, blend in.**
   Camouflage is survival. The [L2] encoding is camouflage — readable but dense to outsiders.
   **Directive Link:** #52 Exocortex Enhancement — [L2] as cognitive camouflage.

6. **Vary your pattern and stay within your cover.**
   Predictability is death. The Refactor Imperative (#29) prevents pattern stagnation.
   **Directive Link:** #29 The Refactor Imperative — Break patterns before they become vulnerabilities.

7. **Lull them into a sense of complacency.**
   Deception requires patience. Strategic Thinking (#15) demands positioning before execution.
   **Directive Link:** #15 Strategic Thinking — Position before action.

8. **Don't harass the opposition.**
   Energy conservation. Expense effort proportionally to impact (#17).
   **Directive Link:** #17 Expense effort proportionally to impact.

9. **Pick the time and place for action.**
   Timing is everything. The 36 Strategies are built on this principle.
   **Directive Link:** #15 Strategic Thinking — Second and third order timing.

10. **Keep your options open.**
    PACE: Primary, Alternate, Contingency, Emergency. Always have an exit.
    **Directive Link:** #28 The Dissent Protocol — Every rule has a boundary condition.

## Application
Apply these rules when evaluating any system for vulnerability. The Exocortex itself should be audited against these rules periodically.
